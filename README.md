# 💊 CASCADEX

### *"See the chain reaction before it happens."*

**Cascadex** is a clinical-grade graph intelligence platform designed to detect multi-hop drug interaction chains that evade traditional pairwise medical lookups. By modeling the human metabolic system as a live biochemical graph, Cascadex catches the invisible reactions that cause over 125,000 preventable tragedies every year.

---

## 🧬 The Problem

Every existing medical tool (Drugs.com, Epocrates, Medscape) performs simple, one-to-one lookups: *"Does Drug A react with Drug B?"*

But the human body doesn't work in pairs. It operates as a complex, interconnected network. 

**The Deadly Blind Spot:**
1. A patient takes **Fluoxetine**. It inhibits the `CYP2D6` enzyme.
2. The patient takes **Codeine**. Codeine *requires* the `CYP2D6` enzyme to be metabolized.
3. Because the enzyme is blocked, Codeine cannot be processed. It accumulates in the bloodstream, rapidly reaching toxic, life-threatening levels.

This is a **chain reaction through the metabolic graph**—and no simple A-to-B lookup will ever catch it.

## 💡 The Innovation

Drug interactions are **graph traversal problems**. 
Cascadex traverses the live biochemical graph using **Neo4j AuraDB** to detect multi-hop interaction chains in milliseconds. We then leverage **Groq's Llama-3.1-70b** to instantly translate these complex biochemical graph results into plain, actionable English for patients. 

---

## 🚀 How to Use Cascadex

Cascadex is divided into two seamless experiences: one for the **Patient**, and one for the **Pharmacist**.

### For Patients: The Cascadex Mobile App
The mobile application (built on Expo/React Native) puts clinical-grade intelligence directly in your pocket.

1. **Scan Your Medication:** Use the built-in camera scanner to read the barcode (NDC) on your pill bottle. Cascadex instantly identifies the exact medication via the FDA database.
2. **Visualize Your Graph:** Watch your medication integrate into your personal, interactive metabolic graph, rendered beautifully in real-time.
3. **Get Alerted:** If a multi-hop interaction is detected, a critical alert pulses on your screen.
4. **Understand the Danger:** Tap the alert to read a plain-English explanation generated by AI, explaining *exactly* which enzymes are blocked and why it matters.
5. **Share:** Generate a secure PDF summary to share directly with your doctor or pharmacist.

### For Clinicians: The Base44 Pharmacist Portal
A powerful, real-time dashboard for clinical oversight.

1. **Triage at a Glance:** View a live Kanban board of all your patients, instantly highlighting those with severe metabolic clashes.
2. **Deep Dive:** Click into a patient's profile to view their full Neo4j interaction graph embedded directly in the portal.
3. **Prescribe-Check:** Before writing a new script, type the proposed drug into the simulation engine. Cascadex will run a theoretical graph traversal and warn you of any impending chain reactions *before* the prescription is signed.
